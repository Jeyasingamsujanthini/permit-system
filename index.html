About Us

The Sri Lanka E-Permit System provides a centralized platform for citizens to apply for permits such as building permits, trade licenses, road excavation permissions, and environmental clearances. This system simplifies the process by bringing all Pradeshiya Sabhas into a single online service, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and convenience for both citizens and administrators.
